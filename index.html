About Me

I'm Liu Ehan, a visual designer and creative director currently studying at Hongik University in Seoul, South Korea. My work explores the balance between aesthetic expression and functional communication, with particular interest in typography, brand identity, and experimental design.

With a multicultural background and training in both Eastern and Western design traditions, I bring a unique perspective to every project. My approach combines meticulous attention to detail with a willingness to push creative boundaries.

I believe that meaningful design goes beyond visual appeal—it tells stories, creates connections, and solves problems in innovative ways.

Project Overview

This project explores the intersection of form and function, creating a visual language that communicates both aesthetic value and practical purpose. Through careful consideration of typography, color theory, and spatial relationships, the final design achieves a balance between visual impact and clarity of message.

The design process involved extensive research and experimentation, drawing inspiration from both traditional Eastern aesthetics and contemporary Western design principles. The result is a cohesive visual system that speaks to diverse audiences while maintaining a distinctive creative voice.
